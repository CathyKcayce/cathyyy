About Us

Our approach is proven and powerful Expanding upon the success of WaterCredit, Water.org offers a portfolio of smart solutions that break down the barriers between people living in poverty and access to safe water and sanitation. We work with and through partners to close the gap in financing for universal access to safe water and sanitation. Now more than ever, access to safe water is critical to the health of families around the world. Together, we play an important role in helping to meet this urgent need.

Mission

To build and implement safe water, sanitation, and hygiene solutions for undeserved communities.

Vision

A world where every individual has access to safe drinking water.

Ojectives

  1. Ensure Universal access to safe drinking water.
  2. Protect and restore water-related ecosystems
  3. Promote sustainable water resource management
