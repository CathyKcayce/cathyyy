About Us

Our approach is proven and powerful Expanding upon the success of WaterCredit, Water.org offers a portfolio of smart solutions that break down the barriers between people living in poverty and access to safe water and sanitation. We work with and through partners to close the gap in financing for universal access to safe water and sanitation. Now more than ever, access to safe water is critical to the health of families around the world. Together, we play an important role in helping to meet this urgent need.

Our Founders

Tim Milne

Born and raised in England, Tim developed an early and deep connection to the state's unique and fragile aquatic ecosystems. He witnessed the severe water shortages during a project in Africa, which inspired him to act. He launched the organization using his own funds and professional connections.

Zhang Chao

Chao brought his extensive experience in large-scale infrastructure and water projects to Uganda. As a co-founder, applied his specialized knowledge in water access construction, focusing on sustainable and durable solutions like borehole drilling and equipment installation.

Impacts

Health Impacts

Improved hygiene

Access to sanitation and hygiene facilities promoted by water organisations is a cost effective way to improve public health and prevent the spread of diseases like Cholera.
All this done through training people, especially children in schools on crucial hygiene practices like hand washing

Social Impact

Community Engagement

Our organisation influences social dynamics by addressing community engagement issues We build community capacity by involving local leaders and members in the managementand maintainence of water systems. This strengthens local institutions and social r/ships
Constructing infrastructure. Building and rehabilitating wells, piped water systems and boreholes.

Environmental Impact

EcoSystem Protection

Effective water managementand treatment help protect fresh water ecosystems, preserve boidiversity, and mitigate Environmental degradation caused by pollution. Wastewater treatment reduces the amount of pollutants discharged into rivers, lakes and oceans, which helps maintain balance of aquatic ecosystems and prevent spread of harmful substances.
